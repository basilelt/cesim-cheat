# Business Simulation Results Analysis Methodology

Version: 3.0

## Objective

Turn raw multi-round simulation outputs into fast and actionable decision guidance.

## Workflow

1. Data foundation and validation
2. Self-diagnosis of financial health
3. Competitor behavior decoding
4. Decision synthesis with risk controls
5. Standardized report generation
6. Iterative improvement by round

## Data Foundation

- Normalize metric aliases.
- Validate team names and sheet structure.
- Flag missing or inconsistent values.
- Compute derived indicators only after validation.

## Core Indicators

- Cash reserve
- Sales
- Net profit
- EBITDA margin
- Net debt and net debt to equity
- Equity ratio

## Financial Health Framing

Use threshold bands for:
- liquidity safety
- leverage risk
- profitability quality
- capital structure stability

## Competitor Decoding

Analyze each team on:
- financial aggressiveness
- market aggressiveness
- technology investment intensity

Track sudden shifts in pricing, production, R&D, and financing behavior.

## Decision Support

Recommendations should prioritize:
1. Solvency and downside protection
2. Margin quality
3. Growth when financially feasible

Always include:
- clear numeric actions
- rationale
- downside risks
- fallback options

## Standard Deliverables

1. Executive summary
2. Data quality notes
3. Financial diagnosis
4. Competitor analysis
5. Multi-round trend analysis
6. Action recommendations
7. Risk controls and fallback plan

## Iteration Loop

After each round:
- compare forecast versus actual
- record misses and root causes
- update thresholds and assumptions
- refine strategy signals
